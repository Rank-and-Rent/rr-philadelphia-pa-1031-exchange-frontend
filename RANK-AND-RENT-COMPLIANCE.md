# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Rules

### Never claim past projects or specific clients
- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Invite contact to share real references upon request
- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes

### No testimonials or unverifiable claims
- No testimonials
- No review stars
- No badges without sources
- No unverifiable performance claims

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers
- 1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property and does not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- Educational content only, not tax, legal, or investment advice
- Always include compliance notes in service descriptions

### Securities Disclaimers
- If DST or TIC is mentioned, include a securities disclaimer
- We do not sell securities
- We provide introductions to licensed providers only

### Disclosure Statement
Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support.

## Content Standards

### Example Capability Format
All example capabilities must include:
- disclaimer: "Example of the type of engagement we can handle"
- serviceType or location
- situation: Brief description of client scenario
- ourApproach: How we would handle the engagement
- expectedOutcome: General outcome description
- contactCTA: "Contact us to discuss your situation in [Location]. We can share references upon request."

### Location References
- Always include city and state abbreviation in required places
- Use "Philadelphia, PA" format consistently
- Mention nationwide identification support where relevant

### Timeline References
- Use "forty five day" not "45-day" in prose
- Use "one hundred eighty day" not "180-day" in prose
- Use "one hundred eighty day deadline" consistently

### Identification Rules
- Reference three property rule and two hundred percent rule accurately
- Include identification rules questions in FAQs
- Mention qualified intermediary coordination

### Boot References
- Include boot minimization questions in FAQs
- Explain boot in plain language
- Reference boot in compliance contexts
